Privacy Policy

Milo Plastering LLC
Last updated: July 2026

Milo Plastering LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information through our website and through the Milo Plastering mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information We Collect Through Our Website

When you visit our website or contact us, we may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Project details and inquiries submitted through contact forms

We may also collect non-personal information such as browser type, device information, IP address, and website usage data through cookies and analytics.

2. Information We Collect Through the Mobile Application

The Milo Plastering App is a field-operations tool for our crew members. It collects the following categories of data:

Location Data (including background location)

The App collects precise location data, including while the App is closed or not in use (background location). Location is used to verify that employees are present at their assigned job sites and to automatically clock employees out when they travel more than two miles away from a job site. Background location is collected only while an employee is clocked in, and collection stops when the employee clocks out. This data is used solely for attendance and timekeeping and is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising.

Camera and Photos

The App accesses the device camera and photo library to capture PPE (personal protective equipment) verification photos and daily work-progress photos. These images are stored securely and used only for workplace-safety compliance and job documentation.

Account Information

We collect your name, email address, and a user account identifier to authenticate you and manage your account within the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify job-site attendance and record work hours
  • To automatically clock employees out when they leave a job site
  • To confirm safety (PPE) compliance
  • To document daily work progress
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To respond to website inquiries and quote requests
  • To maintain the security and functionality of our website and App

4. Information Sharing

Milo Plastering does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share location, camera, photo, or account data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We may share data with trusted service providers (such as secure cloud-hosting and infrastructure providers) who assist in operating the App and website under confidentiality obligations, and we may disclose information when required by law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data collected through the App is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure. We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. The App also provides an in-app option to delete your account and associated data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and to opt out of certain communications. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website and App are not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
